ABOUT US

Who we are

As leaders in mobility solutions, with over a million active subscribers, Auto4Sale is an independent easy-to-use digital platform, seamlessly connecting buyers and sellers to interact and transact in our virtual automotive marketplace. With one of the largest groups of new and used motor dealers and a significant community of private buyers and sellers, our next generation technology has created an unparalleled user experience to search and transact in what is one of South Africa’s leading virtual showrooms.

Buyers and sellers have the assurance and peace of mind that communication is secure, and the platform is run with the strictest security standards. The service is backed up by a dedicated team of customer agents who are on hand to assist with any queries a user may have ensuring all users maximize the benefits of the platform.

Auto-4-Sale service is operated with the same dedication and high level of service that Cartrack’s fleet management and stolen vehicle recovery customers have grown accustomed to since 2004.
